About MNCYN

The Maternal, Newborn, Child and Youth Network (MNCYN) brings together hospitals, healthcare providers, and community agencies across Southwestern Ontario to strengthen coordinated care for families.

Our Values

Quality Outcomes

Promoting consistent, evidence-based maternal and paediatric care.

Collaboration

Strengthening partnerships across hospitals and community providers.

Equity

Supporting accessible and culturally responsive care delivery.

Continuous Learning

Encouraging professional development and system improvement.
